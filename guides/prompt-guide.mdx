## What is a scene prompt?

A scene prompt is a text description of what you want to see in a video clip. The better your prompt, the better the generated clip.

<Tip>
  Think of it like describing a shot to a cinematographer — be specific about what's in frame, the lighting, the movement, and the mood.
</Tip>

## Prompt structure

A good prompt has 4 elements:
```
[Subject] + [Action] + [Environment] + [Style/Mood]
```

### Examples

<CodeGroup>
```text Bad prompt
  A person walking
```
```text Good prompt
  A young woman walks confidently through a neon-lit Tokyo street at night, slow motion, cinematic lens flare, vibrant colors
```
</CodeGroup>

<CodeGroup>
```text Bad prompt
  A product on a table
```
```text Good prompt
  A sleek black smartphone sits on a minimalist white desk, soft studio lighting, close-up shot, premium product photography style
```
</CodeGroup>

## Key elements to include

### Subject
Who or what is the main focus of the scene?
- ✅ `A young entrepreneur in a modern office`
- ❌ `A person`

### Action
What is happening?
- ✅ `types rapidly on a keyboard while looking focused`
- ❌ `working`

### Environment
Where does the scene take place?
- ✅ `in a glass-walled skyscraper at sunset, city skyline visible`
- ❌ `in an office`

### Style / Mood
What is the visual tone?
- ✅ `cinematic, shallow depth of field, warm golden tones`
- ❌ `nice`

## Style keywords that work well

| Category | Keywords |
|---|---|
| **Cinematic** | cinematic, 4K, shallow depth of field, lens flare, anamorphic |
| **Lighting** | golden hour, soft studio lighting, neon-lit, backlit, dramatic shadows |
| **Movement** | slow motion, time-lapse, handheld camera, smooth pan |
| **Mood** | epic, intimate, energetic, calm, mysterious, inspirational |
| **Format** | vertical 9:16, close-up, wide shot, overhead shot |

## Tips for TikTok and Shorts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Keep it vertical" icon="mobile">
    Always think in 9:16. Your subject should be centered vertically, not horizontally.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hook in the first 3 seconds" icon="bolt">
    Your first scene prompt should be visually striking — movement, color, or surprise.
  </Card>
  <Card title="One idea per scene" icon="square-1">
    Each scene should communicate one clear visual idea. Don't overcrowd your prompts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Match your script" icon="align-left">
    Your scene prompt and script segment should complement each other, not repeat the same information.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Avoid these common pitfalls:
</Warning>

- **Too vague** — `"a nice scene"` gives the AI nothing to work with
- **Too many ideas** — one prompt, one scene, one concept
- **Ignoring lighting** — lighting transforms a scene completely
- **Forgetting the format** — always think vertical 9:16
