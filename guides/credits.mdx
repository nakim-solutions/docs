## What are credits?

Credits are the currency of Motionforge. Each successful video generation consumes 1 credit.

<Note>
  Credits are only charged when a job completes successfully. Failed jobs are not charged.
</Note>

## How credits are charged

| Event | Credits charged |
|---|---|
| Job `queued` | 0 |
| Job `processing` | 0 |
| Job `done` ✅ | 1 credit |
| Job `failed` ❌ | 0 |

## Plans and credits

| Plan | Credits / month | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | 10 | Free |
| **Pro** | 200 | €29/mo |
| **Studio** | 1,000 | €99/mo |

## Check your credits

Your remaining credits are visible in the sidebar of your dashboard at all times.

## What happens when you run out?

If you have 0 credits, you cannot create new jobs. You can:
- Wait for your monthly reset
- Upgrade your plan
- Contact support for a credit top-up

## Credits never expire

Unused credits roll over — they never expire as long as your subscription is active.
