## How voices work in Motionforge

A single voice is selected at the start of each job and applied consistently across all scenes. This ensures audio coherence throughout the entire video.

<Note>
  You cannot use different voices for different scenes within the same job. This is by design — it keeps the narration consistent.
</Note>

## Available voices

| Voice ID | Language | Style |
|---|---|---|
| `kokoro-en-female-01` | English | Natural, warm |
| `kokoro-en-male-01` | English | Clear, professional |
| `kokoro-fr-female-01` | French | Natural, warm |
| `kokoro-fr-male-01` | French | Clear, professional |

<Tip>
  More voices are available on the Pro and Studio plans.
</Tip>

## How to set a voice

In the **Audio** section of the New Job form, enter your chosen Voice ID in the **Voice ID** field.
```
kokoro-en-female-01
```

## Tips for choosing a voice

- Match the voice language to your script language
- For energetic content (fitness, motivation) → use a dynamic male or female voice
- For educational content → use a clear, neutral voice
- Test a few voices with a short job before committing to a full production
