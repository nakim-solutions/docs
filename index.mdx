## What is Motionforge?

Motionforge is an AI-powered platform that transforms any video, script, or scene prompt into production-ready short-form content for TikTok and YouTube Shorts.

## How it works

Motionforge works in 3 steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your source">
    Pick a reference video URL, or start from your own script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your scenes">
    Add prompts, reference images, and narration for each scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and download">
    Motionforge generates your video with voiceover, music, and subtitles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Output format

All videos are generated in **vertical 9:16** format — ready to publish on TikTok and YouTube Shorts.
