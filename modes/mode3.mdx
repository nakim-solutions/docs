## What is Mode 3?

Mode 3 gives you maximum creative control. You write the script, build the scenes, choose the voice — Motionforge handles the generation.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Create own scenes">
    On the New Job page, click **"Create own scenes"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your script">
    Enter your full script narration in the **Script text** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your scenes">
    Add at least one scene with a visual prompt. You can add as many scenes as you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure audio">
    Choose your voice ID, enable music and subtitles if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the job">
    Click **Create job** and your video will be generated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scene structure

Each scene contains:

- **Scene prompt** *(required)* — describes the visual content of the clip
- **Script segment** *(optional)* — the narration for this specific scene
- **Reference image URL** *(optional)* — an image to guide the visual generation

<Tip>
  The more detailed your scene prompt, the better the output. See the [Prompt Guide](/guides/prompt-guide) for tips.
</Tip>

## Voice

A single voice is locked for the entire job to ensure audio consistency across all scenes.

## Best for

- Original content creation
- Full brand control
- Scripted educational or marketing videos
