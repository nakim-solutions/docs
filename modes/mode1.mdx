## What is Mode 1?

Mode 1 is the fastest way to generate a video. You provide a YouTube URL — Motionforge analyzes the structure, extracts the script, and generates a completely new original video in the same format.

<Note>
  Mode 1 never copies the source video. It extracts the **format and structure**, then rewrites the content from scratch.
</Note>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select From video link">
    On the New Job page, click **"From video link"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your video URL">
    Enter a YouTube URL in the **Video URL** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Analyze video">
    Motionforge extracts the script and scene structure from the video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the job">
    Review the extracted content and click **Create job**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens behind the scenes

1. The audio is transcribed with Whisper
2. An LLM analyzes the structure — hooks, rhythm, transitions, CTA
3. A new original script is generated in the same format
4. Visual prompts are generated for each scene
5. Clips, voiceover and music are generated
6. Everything is assembled into a final 9:16 video

## Best for

- Quickly reproducing a viral video format
- Testing content styles before customizing
- High-volume content production
