## What is Mode 2?

Mode 2 combines the power of video analysis with full creative control over each scene. You provide a reference video — Motionforge extracts the structure — then you customize each scene with your own prompts and reference images before generating.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select From video link">
    On the New Job page, click **"From video link"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your video URL and analyze">
    Enter a YouTube URL and click **Analyze video**. Motionforge extracts the script and scenes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your scenes">
    Customize each scene with your own **visual prompt** and optional **reference image**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure audio">
    Choose your voice, enable music and subtitles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the job">
    Click **Create job** and wait for your video.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scene fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Scene prompt** | ✅ | Visual description of the scene |
| **Script segment** | Optional | Narration text for this scene |
| **Reference image URL** | Optional | Image to guide the visual generation |

## Best for

- Full creative control with a proven structure
- Branded content with custom visuals
- Repurposing existing video formats with new content
