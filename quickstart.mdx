---
title: Quick Start
description: Create your first video in under 5 minutes.
---

## 1. Create an account

Go to [app.motionforge.io](https://app.motionforge.io/auth/signup) and sign up with your email or Google account.

<Note>
  Your first job is free — no credit card required.
</Note>

## 2. Go to New Job

Once logged in, you'll land on the **New Job** page automatically.

## 3. Choose Mode 3 — From Script

For your first video, we recommend Mode 3 — it's the fastest way to get started.

Click **"Create own scenes"**.

## 4. Write your script

Enter a short script in the **Script text** field. Example:
```
3 reasons why you should wake up at 5am.
First: your mind is clearest in the morning.
Second: no distractions, pure focus.
Third: you get ahead of everyone else.
```

## 5. Add a scene

In the **Scene prompt** field, describe the visual you want:
```
A person waking up at dawn, golden light through the window, cinematic shot
```

## 6. Choose a voice

In the **Voice ID** field, enter:
```
kokoro-en-female-01
```

## 7. Create the job

Click **Create job**. Your video will be generated in a few minutes.

<Tip>
  Watch the status update in real time — queued → processing → done.
</Tip>

## 8. Download your video

Once the status shows **Done**, click **Download URL** to get your video.
